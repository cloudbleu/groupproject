Titanic

North Atlantic Ocean

 

Titanic is set on the unsinkable RMS Titanic. The titanic sank in the North Atlantic Ocean during its maiden voyage from Southampton to New York. The fictional story is told from the perspective of a survivor of the tragic crash who has grown old. Titanic tells the romantic story of first-class passenger Rose DeWitt Bukater and poor painter Jack Dawson. They fall in love before disaster strikes.
