The Hobbit Trilogy

New Zealand

 

The Hobbit trilogy was filmed in New Zealand.The Hobbit tells the story of Bilbo Baggins' first adventure. Bilbo lives a comfortable and simple life in the shire with his fellow hobbits until an encounter with the wizard Gandalf changes everything. Bilbo is persuaded to join a group of Dwarves who are setting out to reclaim their Kingdom of Erebor. Bilbo and his new companions go on a journey filled with orcs, goblins, a dragon, and Elves. Bilbo also encounter Gollum and comes to possess a gold ring which has the power to let its user rule over Middle Earth. Starring Martin Freeman, Ian McKellen, Richard Armitage, Benedict Cumberbatch, Orlando Bloom, and Evangeline Lilly. Directed by Peter Jackson.

 
 
 

Review by Ben Kendrik

"If the Hobbit: An unexpected journey is any indication of what's to come, Lord of the Rings faithfuls have reason to be hopeful that the director will create another captivating round of adventures."

Code Girls Review

It is hard to split a book which spends a lot of time describing Middle Earth on such great detail into 3 films. One could argue that there isn't enough in the book to make three films. It is therefore understandable that extra scenes were added to bulk up the movie. Despite this, The Hobbit trilogy showcases the magic that Tolkien brought to his books.
